About Us

We are a family-owned and operated marriage home in Dadri, Uttar Pradesh. We provide facilities like catering, birthdays, functions, marriage, meetings, etc. We have a spacious hall, a beautiful lawn, and a dedicated staff to make your event memorable. We offer affordable packages and customized menus to suit your needs and budget. Whether you are planning a small gathering or a grand celebration, we are here to make it happen.

Our Services

Catering

We provide delicious and hygienic food for your guests. We have a variety of cuisines and dishes to choose from. We can also cater to your special dietary requirements and preferences.

Birthdays

We make your birthday party fun and memorable. We have a DJ, a cake, balloons, decorations, games, and more. We can also arrange for a theme party or a surprise party for your loved ones.

Functions

We host all kinds of functions and events, such as anniversaries, engagements, receptions, baby showers, etc. We have a stage, a sound system, a projector, and a lighting system. We can also help you with invitations, photography, and videography.

Weddings

We make your wedding day special and unforgettable. We have a mandap, a flower decoration, a bridal makeup, a mehndi, and a band. We can also assist you with booking a priest, a pandit, or a registrar.

Meetings

We offer a professional and comfortable venue for your meetings, conferences, seminars, workshops, etc. We have a boardroom, a conference hall, and a business center. We can also provide you with a Wi-Fi, a printer, a scanner, and a fax machine.
